Gnomonic projection

This map seems highly distorted. Not only it is apparent, but you can confirm it by Tissot's ellipses. However, it has a unique feature: all shortest paths on Earth (geodesics) are drawn as straight lines. Thus, if you want that the shortest path between two points to be (to appear) the straight line, you must distort the map that much.

Besides, not the whole Earth can be displayed, only a small portion smaller than a half-sphere. The center of the projection is taken at our location , but you can choose any point. You can create this map by projecting each point on the Earth's surface from the center of the Earth onto a tangent plane at our location.
